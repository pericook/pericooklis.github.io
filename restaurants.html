-Cento

Cento is one of my favorite Italian resturants in Madison. It is great for a casual dinner with friends or a quick date night. My personal favorite is the chicken parm with vodka sauce. They have amazing different pasta dishes and pizzas. Check out their menu for all the details!

What to Get: My Reccomendations

Pasta:
-- Tagliatelle: ragu alla bolognese, ricotta, parmesan
-- Rigatoni Alla Carbonara: poached egg, pancetta, cracked black pepper, parmesan
Pizza:
-- Spicy Salumi: soppresata, mozzarella, basil, tomato sauce
-- Margherita: basil, roasted cherry tomato, mozzarella, tomato sauce

-Rare

Rare is definitely the best steakhouse in Madison! The steak is perfectly cooked every time, and they have many delicious sides to choose from. The best part of the meal though, is dessert! Their chocolate cake with vanilla ice cream is to die for!

-Ian's

Ian's is the most iconic pizza place in Madison. They have so many different and unique types of pizza, as well as a build your own salad bar. My favorite slices are the Mac&Cheese, Drunken Ravioli, and Smokey the Bandit. They also have great bread sticks. Ian's is super convenient for a quick dinner pick up when you don't have too much time!
