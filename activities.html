-Lake Shore Path

The lake shore path, located by memorial union, is perfect for when the weather is nice. I love taking long walks or jogs along the lake as a break from school work. There are also bikes at the beginning of the path which are a lot of fun!

-Farmer's Market

The farmer's market is located at the capital building in the summer months. It is so fun to walk around to get your produce and treats for the week!

-Football Game

If you live in Madison, how could you not go to a badger football game at Camp Randall!? The energy is so high and it really gets you in the school spirit!
